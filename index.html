Mohandas Pai

Mohandas Pai, Chairman, Aarin Capital and Manipal Global Education is a Padma Shri Awardee and former CFO and Board Member at Infosys. He is the co-founder of Akshaya Patra, the world's largest midday meal program. He works actively with the government and regulators on policy recommendations and guidelines. Mohan has served as the Chairman of the Board of SEBI, and is now a Board Member of the National Stock Exchange in India. He has emerged as one of India's most prolific angel investors. He also leads a private equity fund - Aarin Capital - along with Manipal Group Chairman Ranjan Pai.

Kris Gopalakrishnan

Senapathy Kris Gopalkrishnan was one of the 7 engineers who co-founded Infosys. He is now the Chairman of Axilor Ventures, which is an accelerator that aids early-age start-ups to succeed. Recognized as a global business and technology thought leader, he was voted the top CEO (IT Services category) in Institutional Investor's inaugural ranking of Asia's Top Executives and selected as one of the winners of the second Asian Corporate Director Recognition Awards by Corporate Governance Asia in 2011. He was elected president of India's apex industry chamber Confederation of Indian Industry for 2013-14, and served as one of the co-chairs of the World Economic Forum in Davos in January 2014. In January 2011, the Government of India awarded Mr. Gopalakrishnan the Padma Bhushan, the country’s third-highest civilian honor.

Ram Gopal

Ram Gopal, the Chief Executive Officer at Barclays Bank India, since Oct 2017, is responsible for the performance, growth, regulatory relationships and future strategy of all the businesses conducted in the banking entity in India. Formerly being Chief Operating Officer, Ram contributed to the in-country leadership of India, with focus on strategy and execution. Being a member of the Country Executive Committee and Chair of the Operating Committee, he was responsible for the design and execution of a country strategy that supports the financial performance and market position of Barclays Bank in India. Also being a member on the Board of Barclays Technology Centre and Barclays Shared Services, he had provided effective support to the Country Manager in the discharge of governance responsibilities of the shared service companies. Prior to joining Barclays, Ram spent 11 years at Standard Chartered UAE and three years at Citibank, India prior to that. He is a member of the European Business Group Banking and Finance committee where he represents the firm’s interests. Ram holds an MBA from the University of Chicago and is a CFA charter holder.

Manu Kumar Jain

Post his MBA, Jain joined McKinsey & Company in 2007.
